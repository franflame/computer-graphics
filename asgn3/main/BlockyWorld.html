Oh no! The magical guardian fox is trapped by a mysterious cube, dooming the entire world to an eternal winter!

To rescue the fox and bring summer back, you need to find the golden power orbs, hidden behind the ice (light blue) cubes. Remove at least 7 ice cubes and the fox will be free!

Controls: WASD - up/down/left/right, Q/E or mouse - pan left/right, add cube - C, remove cube - X

Grader note: there is a keyboard shortcut to display the fox, specified in the README
